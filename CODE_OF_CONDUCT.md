# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community will experience an environment free from discrimination and harassment, regardless of level of experience, gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

We value mutual respect and strive to maintain a positive and inclusive environment. Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Respecting the boundaries and privacy of others
- Assuming good intentions and clarifying misunderstandings

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics or choices
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Threats or violent language directed at another person
- Posting others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Publishing others' private communication without consent
- Trolling, flaming, or other disruptive behavior
- Insults or other derogatory comments

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including but not limited to:

- Project repositories
- Issue trackers and pull requests
- Discussion forums and chat channels
- Project events, both online and in-person
- Official project communication channels
- Social media representation of the project

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

Possible responses to violations include:

1. **Warning**: A private message explaining the concern and requesting behavior change
2. **Temporary Ban**: Suspension from community participation for a defined period
3. **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the community for severe or repeated violations

## Consequences

Community members who do not follow the Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent consequences as determined by the project leadership.

## Appeals

If a community member believes an enforcement decision was unjust, they may appeal by contacting the project maintainers with their appeal and rationale.
